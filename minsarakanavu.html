MINSAARA KANAVU - An electric dream.

Written By Shilpa June 26, 2021
Film

In Minsara Kanavu , Aravindswamy unable to express his love towards Kajol . So he look for Prabudeva's assistance in his love.He set up some frame work to Kravindswamy to look herioc in Kajol's eyes. But Kajol starts liking prabu.

DEVA & PRIYA After the dreamy « Vennilave » song sequence where Priya and Deva fall in love, this scene depicts a struggle either to tell or to hide the truth. Both characters are experiencing an emotional battle reflected by a tempestuous ocean and a stormy sky. Priya is struggling to declare her love to Deva while he is struggling to hide his love, constantly turning his back on her to avoid
seeing her magnetic eyes ( kannu paakkaadhey daa ).

Particularly, the climax scene is highly etched in my heart. When priya says,
