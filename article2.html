It's not just a movie for me, it's a sought of Meditation.

Written By Shilpa June 24, 2021
Film

Yeh Jawaani Hai Dewani is by far one of the best movie l have seen. It's not just a movie for me, it's a sought of Meditation. No movie has understood the issues faced by the youth so well & whenever I feel down and out, I watch it and it never fails to inspire me and this movie has always been close to my heart. One thing which makes this movie great are some exceptional dialogues, which you can relate perfectly with your life.

1.Yaadein mithai ke dibbe ki tarah hoti hain, ekbaar khula to sirf ek tukda nahi kha paoge(Memories are like a box of sweets, once opened you will not be able to eat just one slice) We all have some amazing memories in our life, be it from school, college, a relationship or as simple as a night out. Whenever we recollect them, they always bring a big smile on our face and we forget about all other problems that we are currently facing in our life.

2. Kahin par pohochne ke liye, kahin se nikalna bohotzaroori hota haihai (To get somewhere, it is very important to leave somewhere) We all have a small booklet of our dreams and aspirations. But sometimes, we get so much connected to some people/place (basically anything), that we can't leave it to pursue our goal. And that's where, we make the biggest mistake of our life!

3. Waqt kisi ke liye nahi rukta, Beet ta waqt hai Lekin kharch hum hote hain (Time does not stop for anyone, time is past but We spend) We have very limited time in our life. We should utilize it fully, but in the process we should also make sure to enjoy ourselves. Be successful, have relationships, enjoy friendship and do everything...untill the time runs out.

I just don't want to stop)- Meant that No matter what, never stop chasing your dreams and particularly these two lines motivates me and keep me up always when I'm down and out. We should choose a life for ourselves.
